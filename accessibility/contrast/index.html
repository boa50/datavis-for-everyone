Contrast

According to the WCAG manual, a good contrast (level AA) consists of a ratio of at least 4,5:1 for texts,3:1 for large text or images and 3:1 between adjacent colours.
For an excellent contrast (level AAA), the ratios are 7:1 for texts, 4,5:1 for large text or images and 3:1 between adjacent colours.
